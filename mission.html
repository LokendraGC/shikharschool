Our Mission

To empower every student with the knowledge, values, and skills necessary to become responsible, innovative, and compassionate global citizens. We are committed to fostering a safe and inclusive learning environment where curiosity is celebrated and excellence is a habit.

Our Vision

We envision Shree Shikhar Secondary School as a national model of excellence in education. We aim to bridge the gap between traditional wisdom and modern technology, creating a learning ecosystem where students are equipped to face the challenges of the 21st century and thrive in a globally connected world.

Core Values

What Drives Us

Integrity

We uphold honesty and strong moral principles in all our actions.

Innovation

We embrace change and creativity to solve problems and improve.

Community

Excellence

We strive for the highest standards in everything we do.
