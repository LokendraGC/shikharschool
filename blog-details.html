Admin July 20, 2024

Our school marks a significant milestone in providing quality education to the community. For seven decades, Shree Shikhar Secondary School has been a beacon of knowledge, nurturing not just minds but characters.

The 70th anniversary celebration was a grand event attended by distinguished alumni, parents, and local dignitaries. The day began with a special assembly honoring the founding members of the school.

Throughout the years, we have evolved from a small local school to a leading educational institution in the region. Our commitment to holistic development remains waivering. We look forward to many more years of service and excellence.

Students performed cultural dances and skits depicting the history of the school. It was a day filled with nostalgia and pride.
