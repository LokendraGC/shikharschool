Our Story

A Legacy of Educational Excellence

Established over 70 years ago in the heart of Nepal, Shree Shikhar Secondary School has stood as a pillar of knowledge, integrity, and community development. From our humble beginnings, we have grown into a premier institution dedicated to nurturing the next generation of leaders.

Our journey is defined by a commitment to holistic education. We believe that true learning goes beyond textbooks; it encompasses character building, social responsibility, and the development of critical thinking skills. We strive to create an environment where every student feels valued, challenged, and supported.

Principal's Message

"Welcome to Shree Shikhar Secondary School. Here, we don't just teach; we inspire. Our dedicated faculty works tirelessly to unlock the potential within every child, preparing them not just for exams, but for life. Join us in our mission to create a brighter future."
